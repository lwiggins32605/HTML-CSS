Humidity


Latitude vs. Humidity

Humidity had an interesting pattern. At first glance it looks like nothing is going on. However, as latitude approaches 0 the less humidity we see. We can also see that the northern hemisphere also contains most humidity at least for this date. This may relate a little to the warmed temperatures that we see on the temperature analysis.

There are probably other variables that we could look into that would display a more clear relationship between latitude and humidty. At least this analysis created and interesting pattern that does encourage us to analyse it further.
